About Me

Hi! I’m Syarif Muhammad Alhaiza — a passionate full-stack web developer who’s all about crafting digital tools that are easy to use, professional, and effective.

I focus on both frontend and backend development using tools like Vue, Tailwind, Bootstrap, and Laravel. My goal is always the same: to create digital solutions that are not just functional, but easy to use and maintain.
